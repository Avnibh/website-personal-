  • My Outlook

    As the Principal of Govt. sr. Sec. School it is my job to lead our school community through the development of our children. I have a clear vision for our school. All students must grow as learners. Students with learning difficulties must show growth in all their learning, even if that is different to other students. All students must be high level thinkers. They need to be thinkers to problem, solve and tackle issues in their lives. I wish for students to seek and find that creative person within. This might be in the performing or creative arts. It might be in poetry, cooking or gardening. It might be in taking care of the environment. I aim for our students to have the best teachers. I want our teachers to be keen learners. I hope these aspirations inspire our community.
